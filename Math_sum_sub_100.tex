Summary:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Tens are easy in addition! Best friend pairs—1-9, 2-8, 3-7, 4-6, and 5-5—make ten.
	\item To add easily, break the smaller digit to make ten with the other, like 9 + 3 = 9 + 1 + 2 = 10 + 2 = 12.
\end{itemize}
